Backtesting — Every Ticker Has Its Own Personality
You can't use one strategy for every stock. This is how you find what works.

A momentum strategy that crushes it on a high-beta smallcap can destroy returns on a slow-moving largecap. This is why backtesting exists — it's the rigorous process of trial, error, and optimization to find what actually works for a specific ticker.

This report tests multiple strategies on Siemens NSE 2024 data, optimizes parameters, measures Sharpe, max drawdown, CAGR, win rate — then validates with walk-forward testing to check out-of-sample performance. The discipline to test before you trade. The mindset of a quant.
